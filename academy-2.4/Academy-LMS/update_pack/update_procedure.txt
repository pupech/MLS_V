Update instructions from version 2.3 to 2.4 -
PLEASE READ AND FOLLOW THE STEPS CAREFULLY.
Before starting, keep a backup of files and database of your running 2.3 version. So that there will be no fear of losing data.
1. First, Login as Admin.
2. Click on "System Settings" option.
3. Move to "Update Product" section.
4. Upload the file "update_2.4.zip". Which is located at "update_pack/update_2.4.zip".
5. You will get success message that you have successfully updated your application to the latest version of 2.4. If you face any kind of difficulties, let us know through tickets.
That's it.
